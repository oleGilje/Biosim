Welcome to BioSim_u19's documentation!
======================================

Rossumøya is a small island in the middle of
the vast ocean that belongs to the island nation
of Pylandia. The ecosystem on Rossumøya is
characterized by several different landscape types:

* Lowland
* Highland
* Desert

The fauna includes only two species:

* One species of herbivore
* One species of carnivore
